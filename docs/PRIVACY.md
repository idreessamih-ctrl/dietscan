# DietScan Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 12, 2026**

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how DietScan ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, processes, and protects your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

## 1. Data Controller
DietScan is the data controller for the processing of your personal data. If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your data, please contact us at:
*   **Email:** privacy@dietscan.example.com

## 2. Personal Data We Collect
We only collect and process personal data that is necessary for providing and improving the DietScan services. This includes:
*   **Account Data:** Email address, authentication tokens, and account creation timestamp.
*   **Dietary Preferences:** Your selected dietary protocol (e.g., Keto, Vegan, Paleo) to evaluate scanned items.
*   **Scans & Logs:** History of scanned product barcodes, ingredients, dates of scans, and compliance reports.
*   **Meal Journal:** Log of meal types, compliance scores, and date of logging.
*   **Shopping Lists:** Custom shopping list names and products you add to them.

## 3. Lawful Basis for Processing
We process your personal data based on:
1.  **Performance of a Contract:** To create your account and provide you with personalized dietary compliance analysis, meal planning, and shopping list management.
2.  **Consent:** For sending push notifications (which can be withdrawn at any time in your device settings).
3.  **Legitimate Interests:** To analyze and improve our services, optimize performance, and maintain application security.

## 4. Your Rights under GDPR
Under GDPR, you have the following rights:
*   **Right of Access (Data Portability):** You can download a complete copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format (JSON) directly from the application's profile settings.
*   **Right to Rectification:** You can update or correct your profile details.
*   **Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** You can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time. This action is instantaneous and cascaded across all of our systems.
*   **Right to Object / Restrict Processing:** You have the right to request restriction of your data processing under certain circumstances.

## 5. Data Retention
We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted from our database instantly, and session tokens are invalidated.

## 6. Contact Us
For any GDPR inquiries, please contact our data protection team at privacy@dietscan.example.com.
